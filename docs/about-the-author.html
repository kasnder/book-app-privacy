About the Author

Konrad is a current PhD student at the Computer Science Department of Oxford University. His research interest lies in data protection in smartphone apps and the regulation of tech companies. He is developing TrackerControl, an Android app that shows you what companies track your app usage, and allows you to limit this. Further, he enjoys discussing all things technical on my blog. These experiences have motivated this short book.
